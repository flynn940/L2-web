NZ Registered Electrician

Choosing the right electrician is not always as easy as you may think. To help make it easier and safer for you, and to ensure you’re not being ripped off or being subjected to shonky workmanship, here’s our checklist for choosing the right electrician:

  • 1. Communication – are you kept informed so there are no nasty surprises?
  • 2. Pricing – is the pricing competitive?
  • 3. Trust – would you trust them in your home?
  • 4. Quality – is their workmanship guaranteed?
  • 5. References – what do previous customers say?
